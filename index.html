ABOUT US!

Hi we are NeuroToys A company with the purpose of helping neurodivergent people through fidget toys that are medically proven to help reduce stress, anxiety, hyperactivity, among other things. Our mission is to help people with special needs improve their quality of life. The project team consists of Miguel Ángel Garzón and Juan Esteban Ospina both students of interactive media design on the Icesi university and their goal its to facilitating fidget toys for neurodivergent people. Miguel Ángel Garzón who was diagnosed with ADHD at the age of 7 has been struggling with this diagnosis his entire life, so he knows how to deal with it. student of 4 semester of the career and he hope he can help neurodivergent people. And his partner Juan Esteban Ospina who is a design student who likes to help everyone, especially peopl with cognitive difficulties. This project prompted him to create a company which makes it easier to buy objects to help people with cognitive problems.
